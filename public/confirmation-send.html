Step 20 of 20

Send the court or tribunal form with your HWF-KN1-6H7 reference on it to complete the process


Deliver your paperwork to the court or tribunal dealing with your case. You can do this by post or in person.


What happens next?


  1. Your application will be assessed by court or tribunal staff. This usually takes no longer than 21 days.

  2. You'll hear from the court or tribunal if your application is unsuccessful or if they need more information from you.

  3. If your application is successful you'll hear directly from the court or tribunal dealing with your case.
