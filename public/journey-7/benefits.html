Do you receive any of the following benefits?

We’ll contact the Department for Work and Pensions to confirm that you are (or were) getting one of these benefits.

  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income Support
  • Universal Credit (and you’re earning less than £6,000 a year)
  • State Pension (Guarantee Credit)
  • Scottish Civil Legal Aid (not Advice and Assistance or Advice by Way of Representation)


Help with benefits
If you’ve only recently started receiving one of these benefits

(for example, in the last few days), our staff may not be able to confirm your eligibility with the Department for Work and Pensions. In this case you should provide a letter from the job centre.

Benefits with similar names

There are some benefits with similar names. If you are receiving one of the benefits listed below, select 'No'.

  • Contribution-based Job Seekers Allowance (JSA)
  • Contribution-based Employment Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Pension Credit and Savings Credit
  • Universal Credit (and you're earning more than £6,000 a year)
  • Scottish Legal Aid Advice and Assistance
  • Scottish Legal Aid Advice by Way of Representation (ABWOR)
