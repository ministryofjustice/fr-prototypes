Step 16 of 16

Send your claim form to the court or tribunal dealing with your case


  1. Write this application reference on your claim form

    2. AT001-15-4


  2. Deliver your claim form to the court or tribunal dealing with your case (by post or in person)

  3. Your application will be assessed by court or tribunal staff

  4. You'll hear from the court or tribunal if your application is unsuccessful or if they need more information from you

    Your case will not proceed until you send your claim form to the court or tribunal.
