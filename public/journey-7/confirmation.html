Step 16 of 16

Send your claim form to the court or tribunal

You now need to send your claim form to the court or tribunal dealing with your case.

Reference: AT001-15-4

We have also emailed you this reference number.

  1. write this reference on your claim form
  2. post it to the court, or you may be able to deliver it in person
  3. once the court receives your claim form, your help with fees application will be assessed and you will be informed if you can get money off your court fee

Your case will not proceed until you send your claim form to the court.
