What is your total monthly income?

When you paid the fee, what was your total monthly income?

Enter the amount of money you get every month before any tax or National Insurance payments have been taken off.

If you get paid weekly, multiply your weekly pay by 52, then divide it by 12. This will give you a monthly total.

There are some benefits you shouldn't include as income, see the full list and further information about completing this section in the guide.

You only need to fill in the fields that apply to you.
