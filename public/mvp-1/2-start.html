Apply for help with fees

You may not have to pay a court or tribunal fee, or you may get some money off.

Check if you can get help with fees


IF UNDER £3000 / BENEFITS RECEIVED / UNDER 1000 a month:

It is likely that you'll get help with your fees. However, this is not a final decision as we'll need more information from you to assess your eligibility. Read more about who is eligible

IF OVER £3000 / NO BENEFITS RECEIVED / OVER 1000 a month:

We need more information from you to know whether you're eligible for help with fees. Your age, relationship status and court fee will all be considered. Read more about who is eligible

Apply now

You'll need your National Insurance number as well as details of your savings and income to hand.

  1. Complete the application form: ‘Apply for help with fees (EX160)’(PDF 82KB).
  2. Send the completed form to the court or tribunal dealing with your case or claim. See information about courts and tribunals including contact details.

Read the guide to applying for help with fees (PDF 145KB) for more information.
