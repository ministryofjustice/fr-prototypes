I don't know my National Insurance number
You can:
  • look for your National Insurance number on payslips or official letters about tax, pensions or benefits
  • ask for a reminder through the post

If you can’t provide a National Insurance number

Complete an ‘Apply for help with fees (EX160)’ (PDF 82KB) form and submit it to the court dealing with your case or claim.

You need to include a letter from the Jobcentre that confirms you're receiving benefits. It must be dated in the last 3 months.
