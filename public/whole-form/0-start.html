Apply for help with fees

You may not have to pay a court or tribunal fee, or you may get some money off.

You can get help if you:
  • only have a small amount of savings and investments
  • and receive certain benefits
  • or be on a low income

To use this service you'll need:
  • your National Insurance number (if you have one)
  • details about your case or claim
  • details about your income and any benefits you are receiving

Applying takes around 10 minutes.

Before you start

You can also apply by post using the ‘Apply for help with fees (EX160)’ (PDF 82KB) form.

This service is for England and Wales only.

The information you provide will act as evidence to support your application. We’ll contact you if we need to see additional evidence.
