Before you start

Check the statement of truth has been signed.

Check the applicant is not:
  • receiving legal aid
  • a vexatious litigant, or bound by an order
  • a company, charity or not for profit organisation

What to do if the applicant is one of these

Check the fee:
  • was not processed through the money claim online (MCOL) or possession claim online (PCOL) services
  • is not for a search or request for duplicate documents (unless the applicant did not receive the originals or had no fixed address when an order was made)

National Insurance number

If NI number isn't provided:
  • Check answer to question 9
  • If 'No', continue to process without NI number
  • If 'Yes', don't process and contact applicant by phone to ask for their NI number
