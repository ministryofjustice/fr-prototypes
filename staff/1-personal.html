Before you start 11:53
  1. Check the statement of truth has been signed in question 12. Unsigned forms should be returned to the applicant

  2. Check the applicant is not:
    • the list of vexatious litigants
    • bound by a general civil restraint order (CRO)
    • bound by an extended civil restraint order (CRO)
    • bound by a restrictions of proceedings order (RPO)
    • receiving Legal Aid, see more about Legal Aid
    • a company (excluding companies applying to the Gambling Appeals Tribunal) see more about exceptions for companies
    • li a charity or not for profit organisation (excluding those making a case to the UK Supreme Court) see more about exceptions for charities and non-profit organisations

  3. Check the case or claim is not:
    • either a money claim online (MCOL) or possession claim online (PCOL) (including refunds)
    • a request for duplicate documents or search fees (unless the applicant did not receive the originals or had no fixed address when an order was made)
National Insurance number

If the applicant has answered ‘Yes’ to question 9, but has not provided their NI number in question 1, contact them by phone to ask for it.

Status

If the applicant is part of a couple but their case concerns their partner, eg divorce, dissolution or domestic violence, select 'Single'.
