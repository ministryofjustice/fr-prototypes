Emergency cases

An emergency case is one where delay risks harm to the applicant or to the applicant’s case.

Example of emergency cases:
  • suspending an eviction
  • debtor insolvency petition
  • children or vulnerable adults
  • domestic violence
  • injunctions
  • ‘out of hours’ provisions at the Royal Courts of Justice
