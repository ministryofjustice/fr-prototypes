Dear Mr Mark Morgan

Reference: AT001-15-1

We have received your application for help with fees and need some more information from you.

Bank statements

Last month’s bank statement for your main bank account.

Wages

Most recent payslip, no more than 6 weeks old.

Self employed

Most recent tax return (Self Assessment) and HMRC Self Assessment Tax Calculation.

Child Benefit, Working Tax Credit and Child Tax Credit

Letter from HMRC saying you're receiving these benefits.

Maintenance payments

Sealed court order, Child Support Agency Assessment, signed voluntary agreement or a letter of agreement signed by both parties

Contribution-based benefits (JSA and ESA)

Letter from the jobcentre, no more than 3 months old.

Universal Credit

Letter from the jobcentre, no more than 3 months old.

Pensions (state, work, private)

A letter or statement showing pension payments.

Rent from anyone living with you

A letter from the person paying money to confirm the arrangement and amount paid, dated within the last month.

Rent from other properties you own

Lorem ipsum dolor.

Income from selling goods publicly or privately, including sales over the internet

Official documentary evidence showing the amount of income received, by what method and how often.

Prisoner’s income

PIES showing earnings, spending, private cash and savings.

Other monthly income

Documentation of any other income that you’re receiving.


Please send this to us by 1st September 2015 so that we can process your application.

Yours sincerely,
