Dear Mr Mark Morgan

We have received your application for help with fees and need some more information from you.

Please provide the following:

  • last month’s bank statement for your main bank account
  • if you're employed - your most recent payslip which should be no more than 6 weeks old.
  • if you're self employed - your most recent tax return (Self Assessment) and HMRC Self Assessment Tax Calculation. If you’re self-employed and don’t have either of these documents, you’ll need to provide bank statements that show income for the previous tax year.
  • documentation of any other income that you’re receiving.

Please send this to us by 1st September 2015 so that we can process your application.
