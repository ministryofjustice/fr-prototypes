Waiting for evidence


AT001-15-1

Dear Mr Mark Morgan

Reference: AT001-15-1

We have received your application for help with fees and need some more information from you.

You need to send us a copy of last month’s bank statement for your main bank account.

Plus copies of all the following documents that apply to you:

Bank statements

Your most recent bank statement for all your current accounts.

Wages

Your most recent payslip, no more than 6 weeks old.

Self employed

Your most recent tax return (Self Assessment) and HMRC Self Assessment Tax Calculation.

Child Benefit, Working Tax Credit and Child Tax Credit

A letter from HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) confirming that you’re receiving these benefits. No more than 3 months old.

Child maintenance payments

Sealed court order, Child Support Agency assessment, signed voluntary agreement or letter, confirming payment amounts and how often they are made.

Contribution-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA), Contribution-based Employment and Support Allowance (ESA), and Universal Credit

A letter from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP). No more than 3 months old.

Pensions (state, work, private)

A letter or statement showing pension payments.

Rent from anyone living with you or other properties you own

A letter dated within the last month from the person paying money to confirm the arrangement and amount paid.

Income received by any other means such as selling goods publicly or privately, including sales over the internet

Official documentary evidence showing the amount and how it’s received.

Prisoner’s income

Prisoner Income and Expenditure Statement (PIES) showing earnings, spending, private cash and savings.

Any other monthly income

Documentation of any other income that you’re receiving.


Please send this to us by 1st September 2015. You also need to include this letter along with evidence of your income.

Yours sincerely,
