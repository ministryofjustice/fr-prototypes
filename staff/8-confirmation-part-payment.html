Dear Mr Mark Morgan

Reference: AT001-15-3

We have received your application for help with fees. You are eligible to make a part-payment and must pay £55 towards the fee.

We’ve calculated this as follows:
  • Your total monthly income: £1,500
  • Number of children: None
  • Amount to pay: £55

How to pay
  • by post with a cheque written out to ‘HM Courts and Tribunals Service’ for the amount
  • by debit card over the phone li in person with a cheque, cash or debit card at the court counter

Please make sure you pay this amount by 1 March 2016

If you don’t agree with the amount you have to pay

If you disagree with our decision about the amount you have to pay, you can appeal to Sarah Roberts, Delivery Manager at Bristol Civil and Family Justice Centre. You need to do this by 24 February 2016.

Yours sincerely,

Juliana Richardson
