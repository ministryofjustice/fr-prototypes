Introduction

In rare cases, criminal proceedings can be brought against an applicant for fraud if they are found to have intentionally made false declaration(s) on their application for help with fees.


What to do

If you suspect that an applicant has made fraudulent claims you should pass the details on to your delivery manager.

What happens next:

  • delivery managers will refer the application to the HMCTS Governance and Assurance Fraud and Investigations team (by email or telephone)
  • the fraud team will take on the application and make enquiries
  • they will be in touch with a verdict and let you know whether you should process the application, or write a letter to the applicant and post all of the documents back
